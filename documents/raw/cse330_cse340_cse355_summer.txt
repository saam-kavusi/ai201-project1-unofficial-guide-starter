Comment:
Just finished these 3 courses this semester, took gordon for 330/340 and Lee for 355 so ill give advice on them.
The hardest imo was 340, I definitely agree with everyone here you need to start early. I got a 64/100 on the first project bc i didn’t take Gordon’s warnings seriously and started the day before. Next 2 projects I started a week early on and it was fine. Concepts were easy to understand just projects that were difficult.
330 was easy-ish for me but i know some people did struggle, the projects can be a bit confusing but they kinda build off of each other and there are documents to walk you through it. Also is plenty of bonus credit, bunch of students finished with more than a 100% in the class.
355 was more theory and the tests were most difficult in this class with averages of 30ish, but they were heavily curved. So a 30 was usually a B since that was the average. The concepts were arguably easy but truly understanding the concepts was tough. A LOT of proof writing in this class and honestly that’s my weak suit but i still got through it. Just understand the stuff from the first few chapters and read the textbook since everything builds off of the first chapters.
Just be locked in and you’ll be good, take gordon and take advantage of all the bonus credit. He posts lectures and guides from other professors anyway who dont have that extra credit. Lee is also a great lecturer and he tells you everything you need to succeed.
